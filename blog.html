Key Cloud Security Challenges

Cloud Data Breaches

Data breaches can lead to severe financial and reputational damage. For instance, the 2019 Capital One breach affected over 100 million customers and resulted in an $80 million settlement. This incident underscores the importance of thorough cloud access management and stringent cloud compliance

Misconfigurations

Misconfigurations are a leading cause of cloud security vulnerabilities. Regular scrutiny and proper security controls are essential to prevent these mistakes and protect your data.

Insufficient Access Management

Effective cloud access management is vital to ensure that only authorized individuals have access to sensitive data. Implementing strong access controls can prevent unauthorized access and potential breaches.
